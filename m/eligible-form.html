Welcome to the SUCCEED Research Study

We have invited you to take part in an exciting new research study using a novel sleep program that can be accessed via a website or iOS app and aims to help improve your insomnia symptoms using digital Cognitive Behavioural Therapy for Insomnia (CBT-I). In this study, we would like to find out if digital CBT-I improves insomnia symptoms as well as cognitive performance.

This is a 12-week study for individuals who are over the age of 50, have sleep and cognitive problems and access to a smartphone or computer.

To determine if you are eligible to participate in the next phase of our study, you will be asked to complete a short series of questionnaires (max. 5-10 minutes). You will know right away if you are eligible for the next phase of this study.

Your participation in this study is voluntary.
