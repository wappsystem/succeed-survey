Welcome to the digital health survey to explore digital health preferences and needs for older adults with poor sleep.

Your responses to this survey will help determine the digital health preferences and user experience needs in a new mobile application to improve sleep.

You are eligible to complete the survey if you are:

1. Over 60 years
2. Have insomnia symptoms or meet criteria for a poor sleeper

All answers are anonymous and absolutely no identifiable data will be collected. To establish whether you are eligible to complete the survey, please complete the screening questionnaires (5 minutes).

The ethical aspects of this research project have been approved by the Sydney Local Health District (SLHD) Health Research Ethics Committee (HREC) - Reference X21-0265

Read the Participant information Statement Here to learn more about the research study or contact the research team at sleepfix@woolcock.org.au
